Skins[edit]

Base
An outfit commonly worn by Operators.
While not as practical as a uniform, it's what they find most comfortable.
Elite 2
An outfit that has been improved after the Operator's Promotion.
Custom-made to suit each Operator's experiences and needs, these outfits are designed to provide them maximum flexibility on any battlefield. Furthermore, this is all done without compromising the comfort that they've come to expect.

Outfits[edit]

W Dali
Dynamic ArtSpecial Onboard SceneExclusive VoicelinesOutfit Voucher
One of Młynar's casual outfits.
He has spent a long time searching, yet is still where he started. The night before news arrived of a Nearl family swordstaff entering Leithanien, he could not help seeing some long-lost silhouettes through the torrential rain.
EPOQUE Sub-Brand [Passe] Series Selects/W Dali. Młynar's attire as a knight-errant, wandering Kazimierz's plains and endless forests, the words engraved on the hilt of his sword not yet covered by useless trinkets.
How to obtain: Zwillingstürme im Herbst, Ending a Grand Overture and Retracing Our Steps (Outfit Store, 24 )


Roar Against The Wilds
One of Młynar's iteration points.
"If you wish to go, they cannot stop you, but you wouldn't be able to live with yourself, would you? You cannot sit idly by while darkness becomes the master of all. So go, show them how a spear forged of light shall never break.""
Iteration Provident/Roar Against The Wilds. Młynar's iteration from decision point F/3019226. The Black Riders have finally arrived at the Forgotten Plains, but the Hermit Knight chooses not to leave. Instead, he bars the way, alone.
How to obtain: The Masses' Travels (Outfit Store, 18 )
