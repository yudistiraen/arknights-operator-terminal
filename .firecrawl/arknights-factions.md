# Faction

From Arknights Terra Wiki

[Jump to navigation](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Faction#mw-head) [Jump to search](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Faction#searchInput)

The world of _[Arknights](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arknights "Arknights")_ is comprised of many **factions**, including nation-states, corporations, independent groups, and major superpowers. Every faction has their own agenda, and they are all connected by the turbulent history of [Terra](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Terra "Terra").

## List\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Faction?action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: List")\]

\[ [Edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Faction/Main?action=edit)\]

Factions to which [Operators](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") in _Arknights_ are affiliated/aligned with as displayed in-game and their branches if applicable.

Do note that a displayed faction is not always fully indicative of an Operator's current affiliation/allegiance as the stories of _Arknights_ continue to develop.

Attention!

The descriptions of each faction are unofficial and intended to provide readers with a quick introduction to each faction.

## Trivia\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Faction?action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Trivia")\]

![](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/SNS_sticker_63.png/75px-SNS_sticker_63.png?70fd87)

Notice

The following content may be out of date, subjective, or incomplete.

Please consider contributing to help maintain accurate and up to date information on the wiki.

- Prior to the update of the Network [Archive](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Archive "Archive") upon the release of [Who is Real](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Who_is_Real "Who is Real") (CN)/ [Mansfield Break](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mansfield_Break "Mansfield Break") (Global), almost all [Operators](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") in the game had [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island") displayed as their affiliated faction. This "catch-all" status is possibly due to inconsistencies regarding factors determining an Operator's displayed faction.
- After the update to the Network Archive, almost all Operators were reclassified into more accurate factions, with R.I. reserved for Operators that:
  - Were already employed at R.I. for some time (implied or otherwise) before the start of [Main Theme](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Main_Theme "Main Theme").
  - Cut off their ties with their previous faction before joining R.I.
  - Does not have a known birthplace or affiliation/allegiance prior to joining R.I.
  - May be affiliated to a certain faction in a later update with R.I. as an interim/placeholder until then.
- Almost all Operators who are previously affiliated with Rhodes Island did not have the prefix of their file number changed to reflect their current affiliation/alignment (e.g. [Phantom](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Phantom "Phantom")'s file number is still "R109" instead of "VC109" after having his faction changed to Victoria).
- While [Pinus Sylvestris](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Pinus_Sylvestris "Pinus Sylvestris") is a distinct faction, it is considered a branch of Kazimierz and P.S. Operators are treated as Kazimierzian Operators for the purpose of interactions with certain mechanics, notably ![Skill-RadiantKnight2.png](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/Skill-RadiantKnight2.png/20px-Skill-RadiantKnight2.png?54ce1c) Night-scouring Gleam. This is also the case with [Karlan Trade](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Karlan_Trade "Karlan Trade") Operators treated as Kjeragi Operators by [Gnosis](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Gnosis "Gnosis")'s and [SilverAsh the Reignfrost](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/SilverAsh_the_Reignfrost "SilverAsh the Reignfrost")'s talents.
- [Cosplayers](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Cosplayer "Cosplayer") are treated as Laterano units, making them the only [devices](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Device_(unit_type) "Device (unit type)") belonging to a faction.
