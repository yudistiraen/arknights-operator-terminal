**Rosmontis** is a Celebration limited [6★](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/6-star "6-star") [Flinger Sniper](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Flinger_Sniper "Flinger Sniper") [Operator](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") from [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island")'s [Elite Ops](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Elite_Op "Elite Op") in _[Arknights](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arknights "Arknights")_, introduced in the [Fan Appreciation Event](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Fan_Appreciation_Event "Fan Appreciation Event") after previously appearing in [Episodes 06](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Episode_06 "Episode 06") and [07](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Episode_07 "Episode 07").

Rosmontis is also a [boss](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Boss_enemy "Boss enemy") in the [Vector Breakthrough event](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Vector_Breakthrough_(event) "Vector Breakthrough (event)") under the name [Rosmontis, Simulated Sentry](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rosmontis,_Simulated_Sentry "Rosmontis, Simulated Sentry").
