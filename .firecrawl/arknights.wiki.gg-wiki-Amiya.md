**Amiya** is a [5★](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/5-star "5-star") [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island") [Core Caster](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Core_Caster "Core Caster") [Operator](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") in _[Arknights](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arknights "Arknights")_ and one of the main characters of the [Main Theme](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Main_Theme "Main Theme").

[Amiya herself](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya_(Babel) "Amiya (Babel)") is also a [boss](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Boss_enemy "Boss enemy") in the [Babel event](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Babel_(event) "Babel (event)") and one of the possible final bosses of [Sarkaz's Furnaceside Fables](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Sarkaz%27s_Furnaceside_Fables "Sarkaz's Furnaceside Fables") since its Expansion II under the name ["Amiya", Furnace's Finale](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya,_Furnace%27s_Finale "Amiya, Furnace's Finale").
