**Młynar** (pronounced "Mwinar"[\[2\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/M%C5%82ynar#cite_note-2)) is a [6★](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/6-star "6-star") [Kazimierzian](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kazimierz "Kazimierz") [Liberator Guard](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Liberator_Guard "Liberator Guard") [Operator](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") in _[Arknights](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arknights "Arknights")_, introduced in [An Obscure Wanderer](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/An_Obscure_Wanderer "An Obscure Wanderer") after previously appearing in [Maria Nearl](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Maria_Nearl "Maria Nearl"), the [Pinus Sylvestris event](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Pinus_Sylvestris_(event) "Pinus Sylvestris (event)"), and [Near Light](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Near_Light "Near Light").
