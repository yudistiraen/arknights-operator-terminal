# Summon

From Arknights Terra Wiki

[Jump to navigation](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon#mw-head) [Jump to search](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Summon#searchInput)

In _[Arknights](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arknights "Arknights")_, **summons** are units that are deployed by certain [Operators](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") to help them offensively or defensively.

Unless noted otherwise, all summons will be forcibly retreated when the Operator that deploys them leaves the map.
