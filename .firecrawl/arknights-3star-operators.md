A list of **3-star** ( **3★**) [Operators](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator"). They represent new recruits of [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island")'s paramilitary who mostly act as reserves and rear-line units but have the potential to grow stronger in the future, as attested by [Kroos](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kroos "Kroos") and [Lava](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Lava "Lava"), as well as junior combatants (e.g. [Plume](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Plume "Plume"), [Vanilla](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Vanilla "Vanilla")).

3★ Operators can be distinguished by the following:

- They can be [promoted](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Promotion "Promotion") to Elite 1, costing 10000 [![LMD](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/LMD_icon.png/20px-LMD_icon.png?ec131b)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/LMD "LMD").
- They have a maximum level of 40 before promotions and 55 at Elite 1 (for a total of 95 levels).
- They have one [skill](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Skill "Skill") which is initially available.
- They have one [talent](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Talent "Talent") which is upgraded at Elite 1 Level 55, except for [Catapult](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Catapult "Catapult") and [Fang](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Fang "Fang"), who don't receive an upgrade.

**Note**: _All 3★ Operators have been released in the Global server as of January 2024, and their Global release status is omitted._
