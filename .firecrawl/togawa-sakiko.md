**[Togawa Sakiko](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/Togawa_Sakiko)** is a crossover limited [6★](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/6-star "6-star") [Lord Guard](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Lord_Guard "Lord Guard") [Operator](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") from [Ave Mujica](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ave_Mujica "Ave Mujica") in _[Arknights](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arknights "Arknights")_. She is one of the main characters of _[BanG Dream! Ave Mujica](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/BanG_Dream!_Ave_Mujica)_ introduced in [Somniloquium Serenum](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Somniloquium_Serenum "Somniloquium Serenum").
