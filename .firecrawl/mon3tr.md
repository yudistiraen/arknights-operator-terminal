**Mon3tr** (pronounced "monster")[\[1\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mon3tr#cite_note-1) is a [6★](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/6-star "6-star") [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island") [Chain Medic](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Chain_Medic "Chain Medic") [Operator](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator") in _[Arknights](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arknights "Arknights")_, introduced in [Dissociative Recombination](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Dissociative_Recombination "Dissociative Recombination") after previously appearing as a monstrous entity accompanying [Kal'tsit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kal%27tsit "Kal'tsit") and a [summoned unit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mon3tr_(summon) "Mon3tr (summon)").

Mon3tr is also one of the possible mid- [bosses](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Boss_enemy "Boss enemy") of [Sarkaz's Furnaceside Fables](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Sarkaz%27s_Furnaceside_Fables "Sarkaz's Furnaceside Fables") as either [Mon2tr](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mon2tr "Mon2tr") or the stronger [Evolving Monster](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Evolving_Monster "Evolving Monster").
