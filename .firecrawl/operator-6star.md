A list of **6-star** ( **6★**) [Operators](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator"). They represent distinguished, high-profile personnel of [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island") and their allies/associates which includes elites ( [Blaze](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Blaze "Blaze")), heroes ( [Goldenglow](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Goldenglow "Goldenglow"), [Nearl the Radiant Knight](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Nearl_the_Radiant_Knight "Nearl the Radiant Knight")), leaders of certain [factions](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Faction "Faction") ( [SilverAsh](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/SilverAsh "SilverAsh"), [Siege](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Siege "Siege")), and those possessing abilities far exceeding average [Terrans](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Terra "Terra") ( [Ifrit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ifrit "Ifrit"), [Skadi](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Skadi "Skadi")).

6★ Operators can be distinguished by the following:

- They can be [promoted](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Promotion "Promotion") to Elite 1 and 2.

  - The Elite 1 promotion requires five [Chips](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Chip "Chip") of the corresponding [class](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Class "Class"), many Tier 2 [upgrade materials](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Upgrade_material "Upgrade material"), and costs 30000 [![LMD](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/LMD_icon.png/20px-LMD_icon.png?ec131b)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/LMD "LMD").
  - The Elite 2 promotion requires four [Dualchips](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Dualchip "Dualchip") of the corresponding class, some Tier 4 and 5 upgrade materials, and costs 180000 [![LMD](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/LMD_icon.png/20px-LMD_icon.png?ec131b)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/LMD "LMD").
- They have a maximum level of 50 before promotions, 80 at Elite 1, and 90 at Elite 2 (for a total of 220 levels).
- They have three [skills](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Skill "Skill") with the first initially available, the second being available when promoted to Elite 1, and the third being available when promoted to Elite 2.
- They have two [talents](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Talent "Talent") with varying availabilities depending on the Operator.

  - Most have their first talent available at Elite 1 and upgraded when promoted to Elite 2, but some have their first talent initially available and upgraded when promoted to Elite 1 and 2.
  - The second talent is always available when promoted to Elite 2.
- They have access to [Operator Modules](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator_Module "Operator Module").

  - Unlocking the Module requires four [Module Data Blocks](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Module_Data_Block "Module Data Block"), two Tier 5 upgrade materials, and costs 80000 [![LMD](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/LMD_icon.png/20px-LMD_icon.png?ec131b)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/LMD "LMD").
  - The Stage 2 upgrade requires four Module Data Blocks, 60 [Data Supplement Sticks](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Data_Supplement_Stick "Data Supplement Stick"), three Tier 5 upgrade materials, and costs 100000 [![LMD](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/LMD_icon.png/20px-LMD_icon.png?ec131b)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/LMD "LMD").
  - The Stage 3 upgrade requires four Module Data Blocks, 20 [Data Supplement Instruments](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Data_Supplement_Instrument "Data Supplement Instrument"), four Tier 5 upgrade materials, and costs 120000 [![LMD](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/LMD_icon.png/20px-LMD_icon.png?ec131b)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/LMD "LMD").

All 6★ Operators available in [recruitment](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Recruitment "Recruitment") can _only_ be obtained through, and are guaranteed by the rare "Top Operator" tag.
