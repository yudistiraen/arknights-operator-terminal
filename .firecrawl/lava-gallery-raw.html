Skins[edit]

Base
An outfit commonly worn by Operators.
While not as practical as a uniform, it's what they find most comfortable.
Elite 2
An outfit that has been improved after the Operator's Promotion.
Custom-made to suit each Operator's experiences and needs, these outfits are designed to provide them maximum flexibility on any battlefield. Furthermore, this is all done without compromising the comfort that they've come to expect.

Outfits[edit]

Dusk Wisteria
One of Lava the Purgatory's casual outfits.
After parting ways with Kroos, Lava prepared several outfits to make it easy for her to traverse the land, and this is one of them.
0011 New Release/Dusk Wisteria. Handmade in Lungmen, tailor-fitted with special consideration to exotic design elements. The outfit has been improved in accordance with the customer's preference to incorporate Yan's novel trends.
How to obtain:
  • Who is Real Rerun (Outfit Store, 18 )
  • Fashion Review (since Absolved Will Be the Seekers)
Purifying Flame
One of Lava the Purgatory's casual outfits.
It's not just the clothes that Lava likes. She's even more intrigued to the righteous warriors in Yanese stories who smite evil and restore order. This is presumably the reason why ever since she put on this outfit, she has been hard at work practicing Yanese swordplay.
0011 Tempest Series New Arrivals/Purifying Flame. Yanese-style clothes that show off the wearer's blazing spirit. Ebon garments adorned with flame patterns make one's stalwart righteousness all the more eye-catching.
How to obtain: Sui's Garden of Grotesqueries (reach level 95 in Visit Memento)
