Hibiscus has an [alternate version](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Alternate_Operator "Alternate Operator"): [Hibiscus the Purifier](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Hibiscus_the_Purifier "Hibiscus the Purifier"), introduced in [Lingering Echoes](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Lingering_Echoes "Lingering Echoes").
