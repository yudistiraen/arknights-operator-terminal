**Ave Mujica** is a Japanese all-girl [Gothic metal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gothic_rock "wikipedia:Gothic rock") band from [Earth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Earth "wikipedia:Earth") formed by five highschoolers and led by [Togawa Sakiko aka. "Oblivionis"](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Togawa_Sakiko "Togawa Sakiko") on-stage, who find themselves in [Terra](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Terra "Terra").

## Background\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ave_Mujica?action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Background")\]

_Main article: [Ave Mujica (BanG Dream! Wiki)](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/Ave_Mujica)_

Ave Mujica was formed by Sakiko, the daughter of the Togawa Group, who was formerly the leader and keyboardist of the band [CRYCHIC](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/CRYCHIC) during her middle school period, together with her friends [Wakaba Mutsumi](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Wakaba_Mutsumi "Wakaba Mutsumi"), [Takamatsu Tomori](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/Takamatsu_Tomori), [Nagasaki Soyo](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/Nagasaki_Soyo), and [Shiina Taki](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/Shiina_Taki). Due to various personal factors, both Sakiko and Mutsumi formally departed from the band, while the other three members reorganized into another band called [MyGO!!!!!](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/MyGO!!!!!).

In response to the formation of MyGO!!!!! by Tomori, Sakiko decided to create Ave Mujica. While incorporating her childhood friend Mutsumi into the new band, she also invited three new members who had been active in the entertainment and social media fields: [Misumi Uika](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Misumi_Uika "Misumi Uika"), the vocalist from the already famous idol unit [sumimi](https://bandori.miraheze.org/wiki/Sumimi), [Yūtenji Nyamu](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Y%C5%ABtenji_Nyamu "Yūtenji Nyamu"), the social media influencer, and [Yahata Umiri](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Yahata_Umiri "Yahata Umiri"), for her professional-level freelancing as a bassist. Because of their different backgrounds and experiences, members of Ave Mujica learned to cooperate with each others while tolerating their differences, even though some of them have their own personal circumstances.

The visual theme of Ave Mujica centers on [Gothic elements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goth_subculture "wikipedia:Goth subculture") and [dark culture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_culture "wikipedia:Dark culture"). During their live performances, each member refers to themselves using their stage names, which are taken from names of different [lunar maria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lunar_mare "wikipedia:Lunar mare"). They wear masquerade masks with woven patterns of flowers to disguise themselves from the audience, whom they call "accomplices". During performances, they usually refer to themselves as dolls, symbolizing the ways in which each member is controlled by outside expectations.

## Trivia\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ave_Mujica?action=edit&section=5 "Edit section: Trivia")\]

- Like [Laios's Party](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Laios%27s_Party "Laios's Party"), Ave Mujica reuses the Rhodes Island logo as their logo in-game despite already having their own logo.
- Unlike other [Operators](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Operator "Operator"), in their default _chibi_ in-game models, the Ave Mujica members wear costumes in their respective Elite 2 artworks.
- The Ave Mujica members modules missions references their own characters:
  - Nyamu's module mission is to defeat a [Remnant Orchestra Drummer](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Remnant_Orchestra_Drummer "Remnant Orchestra Drummer"), which is her actual position in the band. Likewise, Umiri's mission is to defeat [Rogue Mercenary Bass](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rogue_Mercenary_Bass "Rogue Mercenary Bass").
  - Sakiko's module mission stage is called [What to Be Forgotten](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/IS-7 "IS-7"), referring to her stage name Oblivionis (Latin for Forgotten).
  - Uika's module mission stage is called [Valse "Mask"](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/ZT-6 "ZT-6"), referring to her actual story in the series.
  - Mutsumi's module stage mission is called ["We"](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/13-16 "13-16") which also refers to her story in the series.
- Every member of Ave Mujica has at least one way to expand their attack range.
  - Nyamu and Mutsumi can use their second skill to expand their range by 7 tiles.
  - Umiri and Sakiko can expand their range via their talents.
  - Uika is the only member to use her 1st skill to expand her attack range.
  - Additionally, Sakiko can also use her 3rd skill to expand her attack range.
- In the PV for Rhodes Island Epic, there's a folder in the background titled "mujica\_Live", which directly references the band.
