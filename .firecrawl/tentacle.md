## Overview\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Tentacle?action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Overview")\]

The Tentacle is deployed through [Deepcolor](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Deepcolor "Deepcolor")'s [talent](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Talent "Talent") Tentacle Summoner. While Deepcolor claims that the Tentacle is a strange creature captured by her father in [Sargon](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Sargon "Sargon"), it is actually a manifestation of Deepcolor's [Seaborn](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Seaborn "Seaborn") kin that she can summon at will.
