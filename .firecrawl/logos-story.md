Logos is a unique [Sarkaz](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Sarkaz "Sarkaz") [caster](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Caster "Caster"). Not only is he a male [Banshee](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Banshee "Banshee"), a rare sight within the sub-race, but he was also appointed to be the successor of the Banshee Lord at a young age. His [Originium Arts](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Originium_Arts "Originium Arts") is oral-type, allowing him to cast it by simply speaking or writing with his bone pen. He can bend established laws simply by using his Arts or put curses into device, preventing any unauthorized access.[\[2\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story#cite_note-2)[\[3\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story#cite_note-3)[\[4\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story#cite_note-4) This makes him the one of the most powerful Operators among [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island")'s [Elite Operators](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Elite_Op "Elite Op"). Logos' Arts has been described as beautiful by others, including his enemies. Logos is noted to be the first Banshee Sarkaz to have ever been born outside Kazdel. He has stated that he refuses to blow his bone-whistle, calling it a "lamentable tradition" of the Sarkaz Royal Court.[\[5\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story#cite_note-5) He is also a good partner to fellow [Elite Operator](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Elite_Op "Elite Op") [Mechanist](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mechanist "Mechanist").[\[6\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story#cite_note-6)

### Rewinding Breeze\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story?action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Rewinding Breeze")\]

Logos and his team coincidentally encounters [Mudrock](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mudrock "Mudrock") and her squad, who were being hunted down by the Witch King's loyalists following the events of [Twilight of Wolumonde](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Twilight_of_Wolumonde "Twilight of Wolumonde"). He laments that the lunatics from the [Echoes of Herkunftshorn](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Echoes_of_Herkunftshorn "Echoes of Herkunftshorn") are still studying the Arts of [Witch King](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Witch_King "Witch King") despite the twenty years that have passed since the ascension of the [Twin Empresses](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Twin_Empresses "Twin Empresses"). When told that Mudrock and her squad are former members of Reunion, he replies that all he sees is helpless Sarkaz trying to defend the Infected. Without hesitation, Logos commands the fog to disperse and introduces himself to Mudrock.[\[7\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story#cite_note-7)

### Episode 10\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story?action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Episode 10")\]

Logos is mentioned as one of the participant of Londinium's infiltration mission alongside other Rhodes Island operators.[\[8\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story#cite_note-8)

### Episode 11\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story?action=edit&section=5 "Edit section: Episode 11")\]

Logos's first appearance is his meeting with the [Damazti Cluster](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Damazti_Cluster "Damazti Cluster") who disguised themselves as [Molly](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Molly "Molly"). He then appeared during Amiya's confrontation with the [Sanguinarch](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Sanguinarch "Sanguinarch") and [Colonel Lettou](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Lettou "Lettou") in the command tower where he used his Arts to neutralize the Sanguinarch's attack on Amiya. He then rebuked the Sanguinarch's attempt on their lives by asserting his status as the Lord of the Banshees and stating that the Banshee will not follow Theresis' will. The Sanguinarch lamented that the Banshees has lost their "old ways" of scavenging corpses on the battlefield and blowing their bone-whistle as a requiem for the dead. He also lamented that the Banshees and Vampires were once close, killing their enemies brutally. However, their conversation was interrupted by the Sanguinarch's realization that this was a distraction for Rhodes Island and Self-Salvation Corps members to get the key intel they needed. In addition to this, Logos denounced the olden ways of the Sarkaz, angering the Sanguinarch, who tried to slaughter the remaining members but his attempt was foiled by Logos' Arts. Logos then warns Amiya and the others to strengthen their defenses as the Sanguinarch proceeded to kill Lettou's soldiers to fuel his own Arts. After realizing Amiya was heavily affected by their encounter with the Sanguinarch, Logos tells Ascalon that they need to take away Amiya from the Sanguinarch and if necessary, by force.

### Episode 12\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Logos/Story?action=edit&section=6 "Edit section: Episode 12")\]

After Ines met up with the RI forces, Logos is seen discussing with [Horn](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Horn "Horn") in regards to the route they will take to Brentwood to build a safe house there. Members of Horn's platoon praised his presence in RI's force and they discussed various trivial stuffs. Afterwards, he told everyone else to follows Horn's lead to go to Brentwood. Right after Horn and the rest left, he confronted the Damazti Cluster who's yet disguised as another Molly who noted that he has killed at least 31 of them. Logos and the Damazti confronted each other with Logos swearing that he'll decimate the entirety of the Damazti if necessary. The Damazti then keeps transforming themselves from being Sargonian to Kazimierzian all while recounting their experiences there which ends up with the Damazti admitting that they have problems in understanding their own kind. The Damazti notes that during their time in Iberia they were disturbed by the [Seaborn](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Seaborn "Seaborn"), pointing out that the one thing that differs them from the Seaborn: they are already one united being while the Seaborn follow the will of We Many. The Damazti then admits that even joining the Military Commission isn't in their interest. They simply wanted to pass time. While a part of the Damazti faces Amiya, Logos warns them not to try to do anything to her. Logos answers the Damazti's question of the meaning of new life: to die and rebirth. This convinces them to end their life and Logos blows his bone-whistle for the Damazti.
