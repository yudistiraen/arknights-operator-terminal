A member of [Karlan Trade](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Karlan_Trade "Karlan Trade") born in the northern side of [Kjerag](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kjerag "Kjerag"), Matterhorn comes from the Yek family. For generations, they have served the Silverash clan of Kjerag as guards. This includes Matterhorn himself, who was trained to become their bodyguard since he was young and witnessed the young Silverashes grow up. Even though he could do little to repair the strained relationship between the three siblings, he still endeavors to protect them from any danger alongside his coworkers, [Weiss](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Courier "Courier") and [Degenbrecher](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Degenbrecher "Degenbrecher").

When [Cliffheart](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Cliffheart "Cliffheart") was taken to [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island") for her [Oripathy](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Oripathy "Oripathy") treatment, Matterhorn and Weiss accompanied her as bodyguards while carrying out missions as part of the cooperation agreement between Karlan Trade and Rhodes Island. Aside from his combat skills, he is also an excellent chef, a skill he learned and honed during the years he served the Silverash clan. He is close friends with [Vulcan](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Vulcan "Vulcan"), who maintains Matterhorn's equipment while Matterhorn provides remuneration, and they often exchange culinary recipes and share meals together in their free time.

### Preluding Lights\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Preluding Lights")\]

Prior to a trip back to [Kjerag](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kjerag "Kjerag"), Matterhorn encounters [Courier](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Courier "Courier") and [Cliffheart](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Cliffheart "Cliffheart") in the hallway early one morning. He joins them and asks Cliffheart if she woke early to prepare for a mission, while Courier muses about her not being used to the beds in Rhodes Island, and their recollection of her childhood mortifies her. After learning that they are preparing to return to Kjerag, Cliffheart pulls Courier away from Matterhorn, promising to meet up later in the cafeteria. Matterhorn watches them leave with a worried expression.[\[1\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-1)

### Matterhorn: One Day\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Matterhorn: One Day")\]

Matterhorn walks into a market in Kjerag to buy tenderloin and converses with the hawkers. They muse that [Ensia](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Cliffheart "Cliffheart") used to follow Matterhorn around and that [Enciodes](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Enciodes "Enciodes")'s tastes have changed from liking short ribs, and also asks about the [Saintess](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Pramanix "Pramanix"). Matterhorn frowns in thought, then purchases short ribs as well. After preparing several dishes for Enciodes, Matterhorn stands in the hallway with a plate in hand when he is approached by Ensia, who has not yet left for dinner with [Aurora](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Aurora "Aurora"). Looking at the dishes, Ensia spots her favorite braised pork and decides to stay and eat dinner with her brother. Despite his initial amusement, Matterhorn remains troubled by something.

He brings the stew to Ensia but finds her with [Courier](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Courier "Courier"), who brings news that Enciodes might not be back in time for dinner. Matterhorn proposes waiting for him, but Courier pulls him aside for a conversation and suggests that they should probably not wait. Courier explains that Enciodes saying he will be late is his way of being considerate toward Matterhorn. Matterhorn expresses understanding and offers Courier smoked jerky, only to be surprised that Courier is currently watching his diet due to a recent injury. Courier appreciates his kindness but admits it is embarrassing to put all the burden on him now that he is no longer a child. Before leaving, Matterhorn gives him snowflake crisps, the candy Courier often asked for when he was little. He muses that he has yet to get used to the fact that they no longer need things from him. Courier eats the candy and invites Matterhorn for a drink once he recovers.

Afterwards, Matterhorn joins the table and eats with Ensia. She enjoys his cooking so much that she gorges herself until she is stuffed, not wanting to waste the feast Matterhorn has prepared. Ensia laments that she is barely able to see Enciodes and wonders if there will come a time when she and her siblings can gather and eat Matterhorn's cooking together again. Matterhorn reassures her that he will always be there for her, then urges her to go to bed soon. After Ensia leaves, Matterhorn clears the table but accidentally drops a plate. Some time later, Matterhorn greets Enciodes upon his return and fulfills his request to heat up Ensia's leftovers. As Matterhorn serves him dinner, Enciodes thanks him for his effort and advises him to treat the cut on his hand. He notices the charbroiled short ribs, which Matterhorn remarks are prepared just the way he used to love them. However, Enciodes chooses to eat the stew instead. Plagued by anxiety, Matterhorn tries to bring up the matter troubling him but, at Enciodes's request, waits for him to finish eating.

Finally, Enciodes addresses the issue, telling Matterhorn he no longer needs to follow his direct orders. Instead, he wants Matterhorn to focus entirely on protecting Ensia. Although hesitant, Matterhorn expresses his respect for the decision and asks when Enciodes made it. In response, Enciodes asks how long they have known each other. Matterhorn answers that it has been since Enciodes was a baby, as his status as a member of the Yek family meant he was trained to be a protector of the Silverash clan. Enciodes responds that his sisters see Matterhorn as family rather than a mere protector, and that he can rest easy knowing Matterhorn is guarding them. Matterhorn swears to do so, even if it costs him his life. He watches as Enciodes heads toward his room, but immediately comes to his side when his name is called. Enciodes thanks him for his presence all these years. Visibly touched, Matterhorn simply tells him to get some rest after his hard work.[\[2\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-2)

### Harold: The Veteran's Prosthetic Limb\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=6 "Edit section: Harold: The Veteran's Prosthetic Limb")\]

Matterhorn ends up working with [Harold](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Harold "Harold") as they help the farmers sow seeds. He asks Harold if he knows farm work and is incredulous when Harold makes a leg from wooden materials. Matterhorn cannot help but applaud as Harold finishes the leg. The farm work is finished, and Matterhorn muses that he never expected to share such a moment with a [Victorian](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Victoria "Victoria") noble. Matterhorn asks how he became a noble, to which Harold says it is a jest of fate, just as everyone in the Caster family mourns the fact that [Enciodes](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/SilverAsh "SilverAsh") was a man of [Kjerag](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kjerag "Kjerag"). This silences Matterhorn for a while, but he joins in as Harold and the farmers begin humming tunes.

Matterhorn says he will try to speak to [Weiss](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Courier "Courier") on Harold's behalf, but cannot guarantee Weiss will listen. Noticing Weiss, Matterhorn then points out that he might not need to help him. After a conversation with Harold, Weiss sighs and tells him he can follow him to deliver something to the engineer, then walks away at a speed only somewhat faster than an ordinary man. Matterhorn notes that Weiss is struggling. Matterhorn receives a message and informs Harold that Enciodes invites him to a banquet that will be held in a few days.[\[3\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-3)

### Stories of Afternoon\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=8 "Edit section: Stories of Afternoon")\]

Matterhorn is cooking a traditional Kjerag dish when [Gummy](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Gummy "Gummy") compliments his cooking. He remarks on the wide variety of food supplies Rhodes Island has access to, thanks to its extensive network of branches across the world. While she is trying out his dish, some of the food mysteriously disappears. The two promise to exchange recipes, after which Gummy leaves to bring a stew to the Doctor.

Matterhorn then warns the culprit, who turns out to be [Ethan](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ethan "Ethan"), once again stealing food from the kitchen supposedly because he finds Matterhorn's cooking is irresistible. Matterhorn lectures him sternly for stealing food and his unsanitary habits. Ethan promises to wash his hands properly next time, making glib remarks and noting that the food has a confusing hint to it. Matterhorn explains that he is preparing braised beastment for Ensia, as he had just met SilverAsh with Courier earlier that day.

While Matterhorn prepares fried beastmeat for Ethan, the Savra asks him questions about [SilverAsh](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/SilverAsh "SilverAsh") and his sister, inadvertently irritating him. After serving the meal to Ethan, Matterhorn leaves to deliver tea to Ensia. On his way back, Matterhorn thinks about the SilverAsh siblings before running into Ethan, who hands him a clumsily made sandwich and then disappears. He then encounters Vulcan, whom he assumes has come to pick up his shield. He complains about Ethan's hooliganism when Vulcan mentions that he also walked off with two plates of food from the kitchen. Changing the subject, Matterhorn inquires about Ethan's age, still annoyed at being called "Uncle".[\[4\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-4)

### Children of Ursus\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=9 "Edit section: Children of Ursus")\]

One afternoon, Matterhorn asks [Gummy](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Gummy "Gummy") to lend him a hand in the kitchen and handle one pot, since he is a little short-handed and [Hung](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Hung "Hung") is out on a mission.[\[5\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-5)

### Cutter: Meals, Ready-to-Eat\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=10 "Edit section: Cutter: Meals, Ready-to-Eat")\]

Matterhorn has just finished most of his cooking when [Cutter](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Cutter "Cutter") appears. He has her try the [Kjerag](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kjerag "Kjerag")-style braised meat, and thinks the flavor lacks distinction despite her saying it is tasty. Cutter explains that she is not good at describing things and asks if he needs any help. All his dishes are already cooking and he only needs to turn down the heat later, so Matterhorn tells her she can help [Gummy](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Gummy "Gummy"). Later, Matterhorn is relieved when [Sideroca](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Sideroca "Sideroca") arrives to help around in the kitchen, saying he would rather not burn themselves out, even if they could manage by themselves by going full throttle. Recalling Cutter's incident and why they have been preventing her from helping, Matterhorn states he would rather avoid food waste.[\[6\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-6)

### Vulcan: Forging\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=11 "Edit section: Vulcan: Forging")\]

Matterhorn appears at [Vulcan](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Vulcan "Vulcan")'s workshop to commission a new shield as his old one had sustained too much damage. He explains that he brought it with him from [Kjerag](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kjerag "Kjerag"), and the steel is capable of withstanding extremely low temperatures, but the damage from the recent operation made him realize it had reached its limit. When Vulcan asks him what he would do with the old shield, he explains that it was forged by the smiths of House Silverash and was passed down from his grandfather to his father, and then to him. It is a symbol of his duty to protect [Cliffheart](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Cliffheart "Cliffheart"), as [SilverAsh](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/SilverAsh "SilverAsh") commanded, thus he appreciated Vulcan's efforts in repairing it. He considers keeping it as a memento while crafting a new one for battle, prompting Vulcan to tell him about [Rockrock](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rockrock "Rockrock") and their insistence on sticking to tradition. To Matterhorn's surprise, Vulcan asks to continue repairing his shield.

Two weeks later, Matterhorn returns to Vulcan's workshop and is stunned when Vulcan presents him with the completed shield. He strikes the shield and is amazed by its defensive efficiency. After listening to the upgrades she made, Matterhorn admits that he thought it would be impossible for her to fix it in two weeks, and even believed it would be delayed when she left on a field trip a few days prior. He thanks Vulcan for her work.[\[7\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-7)

### Luo Xiaohei: Sleepwalker Sailing\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=12 "Edit section: Luo Xiaohei: Sleepwalker Sailing")\]

Matterhorn greets [Mountain](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mountain "Mountain") and remarks on his early arrival, initially mistaking him for [Ethan](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ethan "Ethan"). He asks about [Luo Xiaohei](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Luo_Xiaohei "Luo Xiaohei"). Upon learning that Mountain is searching for a missing button, Matterhorn replies that he keeps lost items in a box and invites them to take a look. Later, Matterhorn notices Xiaohei's downcast expression and asks whether they failed to find it. To cheer him up, he gives Xiaohei a piece of candy from [Kjerag](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kjerag "Kjerag").[\[8\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-8)

### Rosa: Yesterday's Morrow\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=13 "Edit section: Rosa: Yesterday's Morrow")\]

Matterhorn calls out to [Rosa](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rosa "Rosa") and [Gummy](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Gummy "Gummy") to ask about the meals, as the patients are almost done with their treatment. He thanks them after Gummy says that they will be bringing them out.[\[9\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-9)

### Delicious on Terra\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story?action=edit&section=14 "Edit section: Delicious on Terra")\]

He acts protectively towards [Amiya](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya "Amiya") when [Laios](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Laios "Laios")'s party suddenly appears after crashing. He is also involved in the effort to restrain the transformed [Ceobe](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ceobe "Ceobe"), using his strength to hold her down so Amiya can use her Arts.[\[10\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Matterhorn/Story#cite_note-10)
