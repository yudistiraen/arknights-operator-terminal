Amiya is a [Rim Billitonian](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rim_Billiton "Rim Billiton") [Cautus](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Cautus "Cautus") who lost her parents when their convoy was raided by bandits in 1090. She contracted [Oripathy](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Oripathy "Oripathy") due to the incident and was rescued by the [Doctor](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Doctor "Doctor"). As orphaned [Infected](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Infected "Infected") were normally sent to die by the locals, the Doctor decided to take care of her while travelling across [Terra](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Terra "Terra") before finally returning to Babel after one year, where she was raised by [Theresa](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Theresa "Theresa"), the [King of Sarkaz](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/King_of_Sarkaz "King of Sarkaz").

Prior to her demise, Theresa passed on the [Civilight Eterna](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Civilight_Eterna "Civilight Eterna") to Amiya and, along with it, the position of the King of Sarkaz. This resulted in her being classified as a [Chimera](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Chimera "Chimera"), making her the only known King of Sarkaz who is not a [Sarkaz](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Sarkaz "Sarkaz") — an "Outblood King" as the [Revenants](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Revenant "Revenant") call her. The extent of Amiya's powers include the ability to tap into others' emotions and exert some level of control over them, and invoke the power of the previous Kings, although much of these abilities are suppressed by her ten rings for her own sake. Her [Arts](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Arts "Arts") manifest as dark black lines with accents of red, and briefly manifested as blue when she mimicked [Qui'lon](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Qui%27lon "Qui'lon")'s Wrathful Cerulean Flame. In the aftermath of the [Victorian Crisis](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Victorian_Crisis "Victorian Crisis"), her Arts manifest as threads of white and pink, taking after Theresa's.

Amiya acts as the figurehead of [Rhodes Island](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rhodes_Island "Rhodes Island"), Babel's successor, where she demonstrated herself to be a capable leader despite being only around 14 years old at the start of the [Chernobog Crisis](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Chernobog_Crisis "Chernobog Crisis"), and is a capable combatant who normally fights as a caster with fearsome Arts. Although many believed that she is the prophesized tyrannical King of Sarkaz who is set to be a harbinger of apocalypse, Amiya is anything but that; she wishes to use her power to deliver salvation to the world of [Terra](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Terra "Terra") regardless of racial or national differences. To that end, Amiya leads Rhodes Island through the [Chernobog](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Chernobog_Crisis "Chernobog Crisis") and [Victorian Crises](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Victorian_Crisis "Victorian Crisis") alongside [Kal'tsit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Kal%27tsit "Kal'tsit") and the Doctor, and stands ready to face the looming threat from within and beyond.

Amiya, along with [Ace](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ace "Ace"), [Dobermann](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Dobermann "Dobermann"), and [Nearl](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Nearl "Nearl"), commanded a Rhodes Island squad into the heart of the [Ursus](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Ursus "Ursus") city of [Chernobog](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Chernobog "Chernobog"). They were conducting an important mission: to rescue the Doctor from the Sarcophagus. The medics successfully managed to awaken the Doctor, but Amiya was heartbroken to hear that the Doctor had amnesia. Still, she asks the Doctor to believe in her. Amiya's presence seems to reawaken some of the Doctor's previous commander instincts and the two work together to break through the riots that have engulfed Chernobog.[\[4\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-4)

### Episode 01\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=5 "Edit section: Episode 01")\]

[![](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/Amiya_Attacks.png/320px-Amiya_Attacks.png?d794b1)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:Amiya_Attacks.png)

[Enlarge](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:Amiya_Attacks.png "Enlarge")

"You will not hurt them, Talulah."

Amiya and the others update the Doctor on the situation as they evacuate Rhodes Island. She tells the Doctor about the Infected, Oripathy, [Reunion](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Reunion "Reunion"), and Rhodes Island's creed.[\[5\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-5) The riots in Chernobog cause problems for the evacuation team, made worse by a sudden [Catastrophe](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Catastrophe "Catastrophe").[\[6\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-6) The group are forced to fight against Reunion soldiers as they make their way towards the city perimeter. Things suddenly become dire when Reunion's leader, [Talulah](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Talulah "Talulah"), appears in front of them. The group would have been burned to ashes if Amiya hadn't unleashed her black Arts against her. However, Talulah proves to be too powerful, and Ace urges them to flee while he buys them some time.[\[7\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-7) Thanks to his sacrifice, Rhodes Island was able to exit the city and return to the landship.[\[8\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-8)

### Episode 02\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=6 "Edit section: Episode 02")\]

Four days after their escape from Chernobog, Amiya and the Doctor arrive at [Lungmen](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Lungmen "Lungmen"). They had received a cooperation agreement from the nearest nomadic city, [Lungmen](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Lungmen "Lungmen"). Amiya participates in negotiations with [Wei Yenwu](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Wei_Yenwu "Wei Yenwu") and recounts in detail the events that took place in Chernobog as well as Reunion's involvement. Wei then requests that they assist the [Lungmen Guard Department](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Lungmen_Guard_Department "Lungmen Guard Department") to suppress any Reunion forces in the city under the watch of Superintendent Ch'en.[\[9\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-9) Ch'en orders Rhodes Island to search for a young Ursus girl by the name of [Misha](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Misha "Misha"), who is believed to hold key intel about Chernobog.

For this mission, Rhodes Island collaborates with [Liskarm](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Liskarm "Liskarm") and [Franka](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Franka "Franka") from [Blacksteel Worldwide](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Blacksteel_Worldwide "Blacksteel Worldwide"), along with [Texas](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Texas "Texas") and [Exusiai](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Exusiai "Exusiai") from [Penguin Logistics](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Penguin_Logistics "Penguin Logistics"). Amiya finds Misha in the slums and manages to convinces her into joining their side by revealing her own infection to her.[\[10\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-10) Unlike Ch'en, who just sees Misha as a piece of intel, Amiya is genuinely concerned over the girl's health and safety. Along the way, the group are continuously attacked by Infected thugs and Reunion soldiers. Amiya is hesitant to fight them and she emphasises the need to minimise damage to the surrounding housing. Eventually, the team successfully evacuate Misha out of the slums. Before handing her over to L.G.D., Amiya promises to teach Misha how to make dolls next time.[\[11\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-11)

### Episode 03\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=7 "Edit section: Episode 03")\]

[![](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/A_Tragic_Aftermath.png/320px-A_Tragic_Aftermath.png?7d508b)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:A_Tragic_Aftermath.png)

[Enlarge](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:A_Tragic_Aftermath.png "Enlarge")

"Is what Rhodes Island is doing truly right?"

Amiya hears from Ch'en that Misha has been kidnapped by [W](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/W "W") and joins the L.G.D for a joint operation.[\[12\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-12) During the mission, Amiya winds up killing [Skullshatterer](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Skullshatterer "Skullshatterer") in order to protect the Doctor's life.[\[13\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-13) Amiya says to the Doctor that she does not regret killing him, but she only wished there had been a better way. Her resolve is further tested when she has to battle Misha, who has taken up Skullshatterer's position in Reunion. Left with no more choice, Amiya is forced to kill Misha. Despite both being Infected with similar goals, the two ended up on opposite sides of the conflict. Amiya begins to doubt herself and whether Rhodes Island can break this vicious cycle of violence. Amiya keeps Skullshatterer's mask as a reminder of her actions, with Ch'en telling her that one day, her room will be full of similar masks.[\[14\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-14)

### Episode 04\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=8 "Edit section: Episode 04")\]

Misha's death left a permanent scar on Amiya's heart. She decides to go visit the Lungmen slums again and helps out an Infected child. Seeing the terrible state of the slums, dark thoughts begin to cloud Amiya's mind and she begins to wonder if she is even capable of helping the Infected. However, upon seeing the normally harsh Ch'en helping out an Infected child on her own, Amiya starts regaining some hope.[\[15\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-15)

Amiya tries negotiating a cooperation agreement with Ch'en, but the latter still sees Rhodes Island as just a group of Infected. A sudden report from [Frostleaf](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Frostleaf "Frostleaf") alerts the two of the presence of Reunion leaders in an abandoned city not far from Lungmen. Backed up by the L.G.D., Amiya leads Rhodes Island into the city.[\[16\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-16) They fight against several Reunion soldiers before eventually reaching Frostleaf's squad, but [Jessica](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Jessica "Jessica") appears to be traumatised from the fight. Amiya thus taps into her Sarkaz powers to heal some of Jessica's trauma.[\[17\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-17)

As they attempt to evacuate, the group encounter [Mephisto](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Mephisto "Mephisto"), who taunts the group. Amiya resolves herself, understanding that she needs to be brave in order to prevent further tragedy. As Rhodes Island fights against Mephisto, the [Yeti Squadron](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Yeti_Squadron "Yeti Squadron") led by [FrostNova](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/FrostNova "FrostNova") arrive. Her ability to drain heat and spread ice prove to be extremely dangerous. Amiya uses her black Arts to cut through Reunion's defenses, shattering an Originum ice crystal which releases cold haze, allowing the group to escape. Amiya's squad has completed their mission, but a transmission from Ch'en makes her realise that Lungmen is in grave danger.[\[18\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-18)

### Episode 05\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=9 "Edit section: Episode 05")\]

Amiya and the Rhodes Island squad were struggling to escape the abandoned city when [Blaze](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Blaze "Blaze") and a helicopter (courtesy of [Swire](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Swire "Swire")) arrives to help them. They arrive back at Lungmen and see Ch'en facing off against Mephisto and Faust at the L.G.D. Headquarters. Amiya uses her Arts to manipulate Mephisto's herd, giving Blaze a chance to destroy most of them in one big explosion. Amiya then faces off against Mephisto, condemning how he turned his own Infected kin into mindless zombies. With the L.G.D. and Rhodes Island working together, they were able to force Reunion to retreat.[\[19\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-19)

Amiya scolds Wei Yenwu for using Rhodes Island as bait, but Chief Wei states that the L.G.D. had never abandoned them. Amiya decides to let go of the matter since they didn't have any proof. The cooperation between RI and and Lungmen still stands, and Amiya knows there is still much to do.[\[20\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-20)

### Episode 06\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=10 "Edit section: Episode 06")\]

### Episode 07\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=11 "Edit section: Episode 07")\]

[![](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/The_Wendigo_and_the_Sarkaz_King.png/320px-The_Wendigo_and_the_Sarkaz_King.png?b32292)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:The_Wendigo_and_the_Sarkaz_King.png)

[Enlarge](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:The_Wendigo_and_the_Sarkaz_King.png "Enlarge")

"I see you, black crown on your head, melting millions of lives, into nothing but memories."

During Rhodes Island's advance to Chernobog's core city, Patriot and his shieldguards block their path. Amiya and Kal'tsit attempt to reason with him, but Patriot does not waver. He believes that FrostNova's death was due to her "blind trust", but Amiya rebukes him, maintaining that her life had meaning even in death. The two sides thus engage in an intense battle. Through her Sarkaz Arts, Amiya witnesses the 200 years of Patriot's long life, and delivers a precise strike to Patriot, halting his advance and causing him to admit defeat.[\[21\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-21)

Feeling pity for Patriot, Amiya uses her power to conjure comforting visions of Patriot's family as Kal'tsit recites an incantation for his passing. However, he suddenly reawakens when he realises that Amiya is the new Lord of Fiends. He utters a prophecy, saying that Amiya "will be the most horrific disaster to afflict the world." To prevent this, Patriot takes one last step to crush Amiya, but the Cautus girl stands firm and the [Wendigo](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Wendigo "Wendigo") dies from his injuries before he could reach her. With Patriot's death, Amiya convinces the shieldguards decide to put aside their differences and work together to stop Talulah.[\[22\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-22)

### Episode 08\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=12 "Edit section: Episode 08")\]

[![Amiya Guard Transformation.gif](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/Amiya_Guard_Transformation.gif/240px-Amiya_Guard_Transformation.gif?fef4bd)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:Amiya_Guard_Transformation.gif)

[Enlarge](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:Amiya_Guard_Transformation.gif "Enlarge")

To stand a better chance against Talulah, Amiya tapped into the memories of one of her "predecessors" [Qui'lon](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Qui%27lon "Qui'lon") and Ch'en's emotions. ### Warfarin: Rarely Serious\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=18 "Edit section: Warfarin: Rarely Serious")\]

One day before Amiya leaves for Londinium, she goes to get a physical checkup from Warfarin. Warfarin says that Amiya is in good health though she warns her about taking off her rings. While Warfarin conducts a blood test, the two reminisce about when they met and how Warfarin joined Rhodes Island. Amiya remembers thinking how Warfarin didn't look like a doctor and how much smaller she was compared to her. In turn, Amiya promises to Warfarin that she'll make it back alive from Londinium.

### Episode 10\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=19 "Edit section: Episode 10")\]

In order to prevent all-out war, Amiya requests the help of the [Closure](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Closure "Closure") and the [Glasgow](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Glasgow "Glasgow") gang to save Victoria from the occupying Sarkaz. They were able to infiltrate Sudean with the help of an informant but are immediately met with a barrage of cannon fire from Londinium's own secondary defense artillery. The group are helped by [Stainless](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Stainless "Stainless"), a member of the Londinium Citizens' Self-Salvation Corps (otherwise known as [Eartha](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Eartha "Eartha")). Amiya also meets other members of the group, which includes [Rockrock](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Rockrock "Rockrock") and Eartha's leader, [Clovisia](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Clovisia "Clovisia").[\[28\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-28)

While the group are taking refuge there, Amiya overhears Rockrock talking about seeing a "white-haired Sarkaz woman" who is the "Lord of Fiends." The description reminds Amiya of Theresa, yet she is unwilling to believe in it. She dearly misses Theresa and she has seen Theresa in her dreams so many times. Amiya confesses to the Doctor that she could feel an intense anger from the Sarkaz burning inside Londinium. Although she is scared, she believes she needs to get closer to find the source of this anger.[\[29\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-29)

Amiya proposes a collaboration with Clovisia to help Eartha break into an occupied factory to rescue the Corps' operatives, one of them being [Heidi](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Heidi "Heidi"). With everyone's help, the mission was a success and they were able to rescue many prisoners, though not without some losses. Later, one of the rescued soldiers named Bill asks her a few strange questions. Although she cannot sense any malice in him, Amiya grows suspicious realises that he is an impostor. She calmly threatens "Bill" using her Arts when the Doctor brings the rest of the Corps into the room. Amiya manages to reassure them that "Bill" is a traitor when they suddenly get attacked, forcing them out of the underground base.[\[30\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-30)

[![](https://arknights.wiki.gg/images/thumb/%22....Theresa%3F%22.png/320px-%22....Theresa%3F%22.png?503236)](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:%22....Theresa%3F%22.png)

[Enlarge](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/File:%22....Theresa%3F%22.png "Enlarge")

"Could Theresa really still be alive?"

The group are now pressed for time. In accordance with their plan, Amiya heads up Londinium's walls where she encounters [Manfred](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Manfred "Manfred"). Manfred mocks her, saying that she could never understand the Sarkaz. Amiya rebukes him by saying that she's read the emotions of many Sarkaz warriors and believes that this war would only lead the Sarkaz to more pain. Amiya's Arts have greatly improved since the Chernobog crisis and she manages to stall Manfred long enough for [Horn](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Horn "Horn") and her army to arrive, allowing her and the Doctor to head to the defense system's control room.[\[31\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-31)

Amiya defends the group while they detonate the control room, which disables the cannons. With their mission accomplished, Amiya and the rest of the group rush onto a train heading towards Central Londinium. As the train leaves, Amiya catches glimpse of a white-haired female Sarkaz that is all too familiar to her.[\[32\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-32) Amiya finally knows the source of the emotions she's felt in Londinium. In that moment of when their eyes briefly met, Amiya could sense an irrepressible wave of sorrow. Amiya thus resolves herself to find out the truth behind Theresa and the Sarkaz's invasion.[\[33\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-33)

### Virtuosa: Himmelszelt und Federwolke\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=25 "Edit section: Virtuosa: Himmelszelt und Federwolke")\]

### Civilight Eterna: As Time Itself\[ [edit](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story?action=edit&section=36 "Edit section: Civilight Eterna: As Time Itself")\]

In a dream, Amiya wakes up and steps outside into a meadow. She meets her child self and, although she cannot remember, decides to look for the people waiting for her. She trudges toward a mountain, never looking back to see the shadow, [Civilight Eterna](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Civilight_Eterna "Civilight Eterna"). She receives clues from a cloudbeast and fails to read a damp letter, which she has drenched with her tears. The cloudbeast considers tears a malfunction for a puppet and tells her to let the magician fix her. Amiya leaps off a cliff and into the air. She finds the magician among the clouds, who assures her that tears are normal and dries the letter for her. Amiya realizes that the magician was crying before, and guilt causes a bubble to spirit her away. It pops in a hot desert, where she finally reads a letter that urges her to treasure herself and wishes her a safe journey. Amiya continues her walk through the endless desert until her knees bend, and she suddenly sees an oasis. When Amiya assumes it is a mirage, it moves, surprising her.

Amiya finally turns back, finding no one, but turns around again to see the shadow in front of her. She is momentarily speechless and calls [Theresa](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Theresa "Theresa")'s name, then confides that she has too little time to wallow in sorrow. Amiya cries, knowing that she has indulged herself, and almost tells Civilight Eterna that she no longer has to appear, but then apologizes. She releases Amiya from her embrace and squeezes her hand before vanishing. The dream ends, and Amiya wakes up to the [Doctor](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Doctor "Doctor") waiting for her. Looking outside, she remarks that they can set off to the forest before it rains, and admits that she had an exhausting dream with a happy ending. She wipes her tears and smiles at the Doctor.[\[43\]](https://arknights.wiki.gg/wiki/Amiya/Story#cite_note-43)
